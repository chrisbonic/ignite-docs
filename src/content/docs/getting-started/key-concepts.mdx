## Client

A **client** is a prospect you're pitching. Each client represents a company (e.g., Walmart Connect, Buck Mason) and contains all the data needed to generate personalized pitch content.

When you create a client, the system:
- Looks up the company via Perplexity and auto-fills name, description, and context
- Generates a unique, unguessable **token** for URL isolation
- Creates a **default package** automatically

## Token

Every client gets a random **token** like `wm-7f3a9b` or `bm-4e7c2a`. This token is the client's identity in URLs. There's no public client list - you share the URL directly with the prospect.

Tokens are generated from the company's initials plus random hex characters. They cannot be guessed.

## Idea

An **idea** is a single innovation pitch following the [7-slide formula](/pitch-formula/seven-slides/). Each idea contains 40+ fields covering:

1. A bold competitive concept (hero)
2. A verified executive quote
3. The gap/problem in their language
4. The proposed idea with a visual diagram
5. Two tangible scenarios
6. A delivery approach
7. A call to action

Ideas are generated by AI from research and seed concepts, then refined by hand.

## Page

A **page** is a broader term covering three types of content:

| Page Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sales** | Problem-focused landing pages targeting a specific pain point |
| **Partner Co-sell** | Joint value pages featuring a specific partner (e.g., Webflow, Google Cloud) |
| **Package** | Curated collections of ideas and pages - the entry point prospects see |

All page types serve from the same URL pattern: `/c/[token]/p/[slug]`.

## Package

A **package** is a curated playlist of ideas and pages for a specific audience. Think of it as a branded portfolio page. When you share a link with a prospect, you're sharing a package URL.

Every client gets a **default package** on creation. You can create additional packages for different audiences or contexts (e.g., a CTO-focused brief vs. a marketing-focused brief).

Package pages display:
- Client branding and partner cards
- A scrollable list of included ideas and pages
- Navigation to each piece of content

## Partner

**Partners** are organizations that Ignite brings to the table alongside its own team. Partners appear on pitch pages as co-delivery partners.

Current partners include Webflow, Google Cloud, Salesforce, HubSpot, Snowflake, Edgar Allan, and Modal.

Partners are managed globally in the [Partner Manager](/admin-guide/partner-manager/) and linked to specific clients during setup. The order you select them determines their prominence on the pitch.

## Source of Truth

The **source of truth** is structured research about a client, generated by Perplexity Sonar. It contains:

- Business snapshot
- Strategic moves
- Challenges and pain points
- Competitive landscape
- Recent news

This research feeds into idea generation - Claude uses it to create pitches grounded in real company context.

## Quote Bank

The **quote bank** is a collection of 5-8 verified executive quotes with source citations (earnings calls, press releases, interviews). Quotes are never fabricated or paraphrased - they must be real, attributable, and sourced.

The quote bank is built during the research phase and matched to ideas during generation.
