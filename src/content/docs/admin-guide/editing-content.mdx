After generation, every idea and page should be reviewed and polished before sharing. The admin provides field-by-field editors for fine-tuning content.

## Editing Ideas

From the client hub, click any idea in the idea list. The editor shows each slide as a tab:

### Slide 1: Hero

- **Hero headline** - should fit in 3 lines at ~20 characters per line
- **Subtitle** - one reinforcing sentence
- Hero image is auto-assigned via the brand image system

### Slide 2: Executive Quote

- Verify the quote is **real and sourced**
- Check the attribution includes name and title
- Confirm the source citation (e.g., "Q4 2025 Earnings Call")

### Slide 3: The Gap

- Three headline lines framing the problem
- Three symptom cards with titles and descriptions
- Cards should use parallel structure

### Slide 4: The Idea

- Headline and description
- **Visual HTML diagram** - the star of this section
- Verify the diagram renders correctly (preview it on the pitch site)

### Slide 5: Imagine This

- Exactly 2 scenarios
- No fake numbers or undefensible stats
- Conceptual enough to be credible

### Slide 6: Delivery Approach

- 3 editable steps + Step 4 ("Your Team Presents" is hardcoded)
- No timelines, costs, or commitments

### Slide 7: CTA

- "Book a Call" + "View More Opportunities"
- Links configured automatically

## QA Checklist

Before sharing any pitch, verify:

- [ ] Hero h1 fits in 3 lines at desktop size
- [ ] Executive quote is real, verified, and sourced
- [ ] No em dashes anywhere (use regular dashes)
- [ ] No fake or undefensible numbers
- [ ] Step 4 says "Your Team Presents"
- [ ] Visual diagrams render correctly
- [ ] No placeholder tokens remain
- [ ] Idea counter shows "Idea XX" with no total count
- [ ] Opportunities Drawer works (clicking from package preserves context)
- [ ] Footer links go to ignite-studio.co partner pages
- [ ] Test on mobile (375px width)

<Aside type="caution">
  Never use "powered by" language in any content. Ignite leads - partners are "brought to the
  table."
</Aside>

## Editing Pages

Sales pages and partner pages use a similar editor. Fields are grouped by section (Hero, Proof, Problem, Approach, Media, CTA, Partner).

## Archiving Content

To hide an idea without deleting it, toggle the **Archived** switch. Archived ideas:

- Don't appear on the pitch site
- Don't show in package listings
- Remain in the CMS for future use
- Can be unarchived at any time
