Idea generation is a two-step process: **seeds** (quick concepts) and **full generation** (complete 7-slide content).

## The Generation Flow

<Steps>

1. **Configure the run**

   From the client hub, go to **Generate**. Set:
   - **Audience lens** - who you're pitching to (Technology, Finance, Strategy & Innovation, etc.)
   - **Presentation context** (optional) - meeting details, constraints, what to emphasize

   The audience lens shapes the framing of every idea. A "Technology" lens emphasizes technical architecture and integration. A "Finance" lens emphasizes ROI and cost optimization.

2. **Generate seeds**

   Click **Generate Seeds**. Claude reads the source of truth and produces 6-10 seed ideas. Each seed shows:
   - **Hero headline** - the bold competitive concept (3 lines, ~20 chars/line)
   - **Category** - e.g., "Revenue & Growth", "Operational Excellence"
   - **Challenge mapping** - which research challenge this addresses
   - **Matched quote** - an executive quote from the quote bank

3. **Review and approve seeds**

   For each seed:
   - **Approve** - mark it for full generation
   - **Reject** - remove it from the batch
   - **Edit** - refine the hero headline or other fields before generating

4. **Run full generation**

   Click **Generate** on approved seeds. Claude generates all 40+ fields per idea:
   - Complete 7-slide content (hero, quote, gap, idea, scenarios, delivery, CTA)
   - Visual HTML diagram for the idea section
   - Matched executive quote with attribution
   - Category color assignment

   Content is written directly to the Webflow CMS.

</Steps>

## Audience Lenses

| Lens | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Finance** | ROI, cost optimization, financial metrics |
| **Business** | Growth, market position, strategy |
| **Technology** | Architecture, integration, infrastructure |
| **Sales & RevOps** | Pipeline, conversion, sales enablement |
| **Product** | Product strategy, user experience, roadmap |
| **Strategy & Innovation** | Transformation, competitive advantage |
| **IT Operations** | Reliability, automation, efficiency |
| **Security & Governance** | Compliance, risk, data protection |

## Presentation Context

Presentation context is optional free-text that shapes generation. Examples:

- "Meeting with the CTO next Thursday, they're evaluating cloud migration partners"
- "This is for a trade show booth - keep ideas high-level and visual"
- "The prospect mentioned they're struggling with data silos across 12 acquisitions"

Presentation context is per-run (not saved to the client). Set it each time for the specific audience.

<Aside type="tip">
**Write the hero headline carefully.** The h1 must fit in 3 lines at ~20 characters per line in uppercase Urbanist font at 5rem. Longer lines wrap badly on desktop. Tighter is always better.
</Aside>

## After Generation

Generated ideas appear in the client's idea list. Each idea is immediately live on the pitch site after Webflow CMS publishing. Go to [Editing Content](/admin-guide/editing-content/) to polish individual ideas.
