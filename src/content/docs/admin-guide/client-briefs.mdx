Client Briefs are strategic memos generated for each client. They synthesize research, intelligence, and brand context into a polished, client-facing document - the kind of thing you'd send before a first meeting to demonstrate you've done your homework.

## When to Use Briefs

- Before a first meeting - show you understand their business
- As a leave-behind after a discovery call
- To align your team on client context before a pitch
- As the opening piece in a package alongside idea pitches

## Generating a Brief

1. From the client hub, go to **Generate Brief**
2. Review the context panel showing what the AI will use:
   - Research (source of truth)
   - Intelligence entries and notes
   - Brand context
   - Selected partners
3. Configure the generation direction (defaults to "Innovation Led Growth")
4. Click **Generate Brief**

The brief is generated via the compute service and written to the CMS as a page with type `client-brief`.

<Aside type="tip">
Briefs are better with more context. Run research, add intelligence entries, and save your brand context before generating. The brief pulls from all three.
</Aside>

## Brief Structure

Each brief is a 12-section strategic memo:

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| **Executive Summary** | 2-3 paragraph overview of the opportunity |
| **Company Understanding** | What they do, market position, scale |
| **Strategic Landscape** | Industry trends and pressures they face |
| **Key Challenges** | Specific problems identified from research + intelligence |
| **Innovation Opportunities** | Where they can gain advantage |
| **Proposed Approach** | How you'd work together |
| **Studio Capabilities** | Relevant capabilities matched to their needs |
| **Partner Ecosystem** | Partners you'd bring to the table |
| **Proof Points** | Relevant client outcomes and metrics |
| **Engagement Model** | How the engagement would work |
| **Recommended Next Steps** | Concrete actions to move forward |
| **About Ignite Studio** | Brief company overview |

## Editing a Brief

After generation, click **Edit** on the brief from the client's Pages tab. The brief editor has the same two-column layout as AI Context:

- **Left: Markdown editor** - edit the full brief text
- **Right: Live preview** - see the formatted output

Changes save directly to the CMS.

## How Briefs Appear on the Pitch Site

Briefs render with a dedicated layout - white background with a table of contents sidebar for easy navigation. They sit inside the standard pitch navigation so prospects can move between the brief and other pages in the package.

All briefs serve from the same URL pattern as other pages: `/c/[token]/p/[slug]`.

## Adding Briefs to Packages

Briefs appear in the package drawer alongside ideas, sales pages, and partner pages. Add them to a package to include them in the curated experience you share with prospects.
