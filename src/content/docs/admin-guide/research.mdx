Research is the foundation of every pitch. Before generating ideas, you need a solid **source of truth** about the client's business, challenges, and strategic direction.

## Running Research

1. From the client hub, click **Research**
2. The company URL is pre-filled from client setup
3. Click **Run Research** - this sends the URL to Perplexity Sonar for a deep analysis

The research streams back in real-time as structured sections.

## Research Sections

Perplexity returns organized research in these sections:

| Section | What It Contains |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Business Snapshot** | What the company does, market position, key metrics |
| **Strategic Moves** | Recent initiatives, acquisitions, product launches |
| **Challenges** | Pain points, operational friction, market pressures |
| **Competitive Landscape** | Key competitors and differentiators |
| **Recent News** | Latest press, earnings, announcements |

Each section appears as an editable card in the UI. You can:
- **Edit** any section to refine or add context
- **Delete** sections that aren't relevant
- **Reorder** sections by priority

## Building the Quote Bank

The research also produces a **quote bank** - 5-8 verified executive quotes with source citations.

<Aside type="caution">
Executive quotes **must be real and verified**. Every quote needs:
- The exact quote text
- Speaker name and title
- Source (e.g., "Q4 2025 Earnings Call", "March 2026 Press Release")

Never fabricate or paraphrase quotes attributed to named executives. If no executive quotes exist, use industry analyst quotes or market data points with sources.
</Aside>

Good sources for quotes:
- Quarterly earnings call transcripts
- Press releases
- Conference keynotes
- Published interviews
- Annual reports

## How Research Feeds Generation

The source of truth and quote bank are saved to the client record. When you generate ideas:

1. Claude reads the full source of truth to understand the company
2. Seeds are mapped to specific challenges from the research
3. Executive quotes from the quote bank are matched to relevant ideas
4. The presentation context (if set) shapes the audience lens

Better research = better ideas. Take the time to curate the source of truth before generating.

## Re-Running Research

You can run research multiple times. Each run overwrites the previous source of truth. If you've made manual edits to the research, they'll be replaced - so save any custom additions before re-running.
